Tea varieties

This table shows the oxydation levels for different varieties of teas and provides a suggestion for how long one should wait after pouring the drink and before drinking it to allow for optimised release of the tea properties in the hot water.
Tea Oxydation level How much time before drinking
Pu er (普洱茶) Very high 1-2 min
Black (紅茶) High 1-3 min
Oolong (烏龍茶) Medium to high 2-5 min
Green (綠茶) Medium 3-6 min
White (白茶) Low 6-8 min
